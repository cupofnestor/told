About

Owner and producer Rebecca Rideout has thirteen years of experience shooting, editing, researching and producing non-fiction films.


Rideout has produced videos for universities, non-profits, businesses, innovators, practitioners, and artisans in the Pioneer Valley region and beyond since 2012.

Previously, she worked on dozens of films for museums and learning centers with Monadnock Media, Inc., played various roles for multiple PBS-broadcast productions with Florentine Films / Hott Productions, and co-produced Feeding Frenzy, a feature-length documentary for the Media Education Foundation.
